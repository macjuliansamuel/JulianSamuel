Who I Am.


I'm a Product Designer currently investing my time at WolfEdge Capital, dwelling in the world of cryptocurrency and blockchain crafting simple and intutive products

I graduated from Anna University in September 2020, with a major in Computer Science and Engineering. During my undergrad, I was fascinated by Human-Computer Interaction. This led me to explore the field of UX, and after a few side projects, I fell in love with the art of problem solving.

I believe in the designer as a problem solver. Today, I am living in India, but my aim is to break borders to embrace as many cultures as I can and design for everyone everywhere.
